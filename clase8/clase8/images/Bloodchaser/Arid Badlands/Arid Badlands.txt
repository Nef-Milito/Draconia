*The scourge of caravans and traders hoping to cross the north-eastern deserts. When it is not making short work of supplies and luxury goods, it likes to bathe in the sun, contemplating its barren kingdom.*

Arid Badlands is a stunning white, red, and orange dragon. Its body is primarily white with splotches of red weaving through it, before the white trails off into fiery orange along the wings and tail. Hints of orange are found along its underside and legs. Its eyes are a blood red.
