*Capable of staying underwater for prolonged periods of time, this mysterious clade is seldom seen by humans. To the most superstitious, it protects the shore from vicious sea monster. To fishermen who call it "water chicken", it is so fainthearted it probably feeds on algae. The latter usually start holding their tongue when they see a specimen overpower a squid twice its size.*

Rayless Depths is a black dragon with a faint blue pattern.
