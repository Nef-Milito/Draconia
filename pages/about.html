About Draconia

Who are we?

We are the only company in the world authorized to raise and translate Dragons all across the globe. Our headquarters is located in Örebro, Sweden and we count with 3 different breeding centers located in Sweden, Norway, and Germany. Our team specializes in raising and training Dragons from the very moment the eggs are laid, until they are adopted.

Vision

We have a boundless passion for Dragons and we are unwaveringly committed to fostering Dragon-Human relationships, by raising them very carefully, showing them love, discipline and dedication. We trust that one day this careful treatment will make people see them as loyal partners, and not just a threat or war weapon.

Mission

We are dedicated to making fantasy become reality, by giving Humanity the possibility to tie bonds with these wonderful and faithful creatures, not just by adopting them, but also by raising them and developing an everlasting fellowship.

Why should I adopt a Dragon?

Dragons are the most loyal and intelligent animals in our world, as they arrived long before we ever set foot on The Earth. They are ancient creatures dating back from even before the Jurassic Period, more than 65 million years ago. They carry not only our planet's history, but also the meaning of life itself.
Befriending a Dragon is no easy task, for they are very territorial and ferocious creatures that won't hesitate to defend themselves the moment they come across a potential threat. However, if you do manage to develop a friendship with one of them, you will not only be acquiring a very valuable asset, but you will also be building an exceptionally strong relationship which no one will ever be able to shatter. You might teach your Dragon a lot of things, but they will most definitely teach you as well, for they are Life's most important teachers.
