Why?

Dragons are the most loyal and intelligent animals in our world, as they arrived long before we ever set foot on The Earth. They are ancient creatures dating back from even before the Jurassic Period, more than 65 million years ago. They carry not only our planet's history, but also the meaning of life itself.
Befriending a Dragon is no easy task, for they are very territorial and ferocious creatures that won't hesitate to defend themselves the moment they come across a potential threat. However, if you do manage to develop a friendship with one of them, you will not only be acquiring a very valuable asset, but you will also be building an exceptionally strong relationship which no one will ever be able to shatter. You might teach your Dragon a lot of things, but they will most definitely teach you as well, for they are Life's most important teachers.

About

As the only company authorized to raise and translate Dragons all across the world, we are strongly committed to fostering Dragon-Human relationships by providing our customers with the possibility to choose between a wide variety of different Dragon breeds that will best adapt to their conditions. We are dedicated to making fantasy become reality, by giving Humanity the possibility to tie bonds with these wonderful and faithful creatures, not just by adopting them, but also by raising them and developing an everlasting fellowship.
